About the App

What is Aumazing?

🧩

Bridging Home & Therapy

Aumazing helps parents guide their children with early-stage ASD through structured, gamified activities at home — supplementing professional therapy with daily, fun-filled learning.

ABA-Based TEACCH Approach Visual Learning
2–6 Target Age Group
